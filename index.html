Azalea the chimpanzee

Azalea is a North Korean chimpanzee who is famous for her smoking habit. She is able to light her own cigarettes with a lighter, as well as by touching a cigarette to the end of another lit cigarette, though she reportedly does not inhale when she smokes.

Her smoking habit is encouraged by the zoo workers who look after her, as well as the general North Korean public, many of whom find her funny.

Smoking is widespread in North Korea - approximately 45% of adult men smoke regularly, and 30% of adult men die of tobacco related illnesses.

Korea Central Zoo has been criticised by animal rights groups who say it is cruel to addict a chimpanzee to tobacco for human amusement and that smoking is as dangerous to Azalea as it is to humans.

Quick facts about Azalea

Age

Azalea was born in 1996 making her between 25-26 years old.

Cigarettes

Azalea smokes one pack of cigarettes per day.

Other skills

Azalea can also dance, bow and touch her nose on command.

Name

Azalea is also known by her Korean name, Dallae.
